📌 Good to know
  • Our SLA to respond to any query or grievance is 24–48 hours (business days, working hours).
  • We keep a basic billing record (name, phone number, transaction ID) securely in-house for 6 months, then delete it — retained longer only where required by regulation.
  • Any grievance raised beyond 6 months of the order may not be valid, as the related records are deleted after that period.
  • We're transparent — we never push you to buy extra services, and we never spam or harass.
