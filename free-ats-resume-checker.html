FAQ

ATS resume checker — FAQs

Is the ATS resume checker free?

Yes — it's 100% free with no sign-up. Upload your resume and get your ATS score instantly.

Is my resume uploaded or stored anywhere?

No. Your resume is read and scored entirely inside your browser. The file is never uploaded to our servers or stored anywhere — closing the tab removes it from memory.

What is an ATS score?

An ATS (Applicant Tracking System) score estimates how well recruiters' software can read and rank your resume, based on sections, keywords, formatting, dates and measurable impact.

Which file formats can I upload?

PDF, DOCX or TXT up to 5 MB. Text-based PDFs work best — scanned or image-only PDFs can't be read.

How accurate is the ATS score?

It's a guidance score from our own heuristic checks — not tied to any specific employer's ATS. Use it to spot and fix the obvious issues that get resumes auto-rejected.
