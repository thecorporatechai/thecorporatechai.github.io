✍️ Resume Rewrite & Modify — how it works

  1. Payment first. Pay the full agreed amount, or a 50% advance to get started. No payment, no start.
  2. Share details. Once paid, send your reference resume and any additional change details. We then begin working.
  3. Draft review. When ready, we share a watermarked draft. All edits must be confirmed at the draft stage.
  4. Delivery. After you confirm and pay the balance, we deliver — in both editable + PDF, with gift and ATS score as applicable, and the rest as per your selected plan.
  5. Post-delivery edit. After delivery confirmation, you get one edit request within 24 hours — no changes after that.

📊 About the ATS score

Any ATS score we share is based on our own in-house 6-pillar logic (Parseability & Contact, Section Structure, Content Quality & Impact, Keywords & Skills, Formatting & ATS Safety, Readability). There is no official or central ATS benchmark — every tool uses its own logic, and so do we. It's for guidance only.

💰 Pricing & scope

  • The ₹199* rewrite applies to one selected template/format only — not multiple formats.
  • Photo-based resumes start from ₹250 (added complexity) — the price is informed & agreed before we start.
  • We make all resume types, including global / international formats, on request — charges vary as per the agreement made on WhatsApp or any mode of communication.

⚠️ Advance paid but order not completed

If you review the draft but stop responding and don't complete payment, we may contact you across multiple channels for confirmation. If you don't confirm, the 50% advance is non-refundable and the balance stays payable as agreed. We never spam or harass — but we may send a first written warning (email/phone) and, if unanswered, act only through lawful channels. On full payment you get a delivery receipt.

🎨 Custom designs

Designs are inspired by Canva. We can also redesign a model not shown in our listing, as per your requirements.

🔒 Stored securely, then deleted

While we hold it, your reference resume, delivered resume and bills are stored securely in-house under enforced security controls (encryption, antivirus and restricted access) — and we are accountable for protecting them from breach.

Once delivery is sent and confirmed, within 24 hours we delete your information (reference & delivered resume). We do not share it with any third party; if a third party is ever genuinely required, we take your consent first. Billing records are kept for 6 months (see Privacy).
