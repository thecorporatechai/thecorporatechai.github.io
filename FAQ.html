The Corporate Chai helps you get noticed, get hired and grow — with free career tools (like the ATS resume checker and 1500+ verified HR contacts) and affordable, honest services such as resume rewrites, LinkedIn & Naukri optimization, and resume building.

Is the ATS resume checker really free?

Yes — 100% free with no sign-up. Upload your resume and get an instant ATS score out of 100 with the key issues to fix.

Do you store my resume or personal data?

No. The ATS checker reads and scores your resume entirely in your browser — it's never uploaded. For services, any details you share are deleted within 24 hours of delivery; only your phone number is kept as customer identification. While we hold your reference resume and bills, they're stored securely in-house under enforced controls (encryption, antivirus, restricted access) — we're accountable for protecting them from breach. We align with ISO/IEC 27701 & India's DPDP Act, 2023 (self-aligned, not officially certified). See our Privacy Policy.

Existing resume modify (₹99*), ATS resume report (₹19), 5 ATS resume templates (₹49), ATS resume rewrite (from ₹199* per single page), Resume + Naukri optimization, complete career boost, and more. Full list on the services page, and exactly how each works on the Services FAQ.

How does your ATS score work? Is it an official benchmark?

Our ATS score comes from our own in-house tool, built on 6 pillars: Parseability & Contact, Section Structure, Content Quality & Impact, Keywords & Skills, Formatting & ATS Safety, and Readability. There is no official or central ATS benchmark anywhere — every tool uses its own logic, and so do we. Our score is for guidance only. See Terms.

Are the templates editable? Are they from Canva?

The ₹49 pack gives you editable Word (.docx) versions of any 5 templates of your choice — a DIY option you fill in yourself. All designs are inspired by Canva; we've redesigned them ourselves and give you our editable file. The original Canva designs remain Canva's IP — you receive our redesign, not Canva's IP.
